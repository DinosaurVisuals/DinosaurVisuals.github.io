Lecture 1: Intro & Web Dev101

The content was clearly explained and well-presented. Before this class, I never understood the distinctions between Python, HTML, CSS, and other programming languages - they were all just coding-related concepts to me. However, after attending the first lecture, I finally gained a clear understanding of their relationships and differences. This has been one of my most valuable coding learning experiences throughout my academic journey.
My only challenge during the class was the pace of instruction, as English is not my first language. Sometimes I needed additional time to process each word. However, I don't consider this a significant issue since I can always review the lecture materials afterward to catch up on any parts I didn't fully grasp during class.
